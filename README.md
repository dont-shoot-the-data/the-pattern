# The Elephant in the Room

**California just released its six yearly crime reports for 2025. What they actually show will alarm you. Below is what the mainstream is hiding.** Published July 1, 2026.

**Live site: [dont-shoot-the-data.github.io/the-pattern](https://dont-shoot-the-data.github.io/the-pattern/)**

All six California DOJ 2025 annual reports, read together on one page, in the order the state published them. Every number comes from the state's own PDFs, and where a report omits a variable, federal data is charted in its place, labeled and sourced.

## The structure

- **01 Homicide in California**: 2025 felony suspects by race vs population (homicide, rape, robbery, kidnapping) and the arrest-to-conviction funnel; the 79.3% clearance surge is flagged in the report index
- **02 Crime in California, 25 years**: the full statewide arrest series 2000 to 2025 with the 2015 launch of Kamala Harris' mandated police implicit-bias training marked on the line. Black homicide arrest share averaged 4.2x population in the fifteen years before the training, 4.7x in the decade after, 4.9x in 2025 itself
- **03 Juvenile suspects**: Table 33 against California's under-18 population
- **04 Hate crime**: 1,955 events, 1 murder, 0 rapes, and a 3.7% events-to-conviction rate. 1,912 suspects counted, none broken down by race. The only report given its own press release, on the same morning the state's CA vs Hate hotline quietly shut down
- **05 Who actually kills whom**: the victim-race by suspect-race cross-tab no California report prints, built from the FBI's Supplementary Homicide Reports for every solved California case since 1981, plus T-CLEAR's ten years of verified data on who kills transgender people
- **06 Use of force**: 127 civilian deaths published by race with no armed/unarmed split, against the national benchmark that 1 in 18 people killed by police is unarmed and more officers die by gunfire each year than unarmed people of any single race
- **07 SB 54 and the juvenile pipeline**
- **08 The Pattern**: eight documented instances, each tagged to its section, of precision where the numbers flatter and generality where they don't

## Method

Suspects, never victims. Hispanic is never folded into White. Disproportionality = arrest share divided by population share, displayed to two decimals with no favorable rounding anywhere. A source modal links all 26 annual Crime in California reports for line-by-line fact-checking, and the six 2025 reports are linked at the top of the page.
